This project is heavily insperied by one of my favorite music producer, n-buna and his first studio album which called "Flowers and Syrup, The Last Train" in English. This album where the tracklist is sorted by time with the first track happening in the morning and the last one happening at daybreak in the next morning. With an website, I want to recreate that experience with a message that even when things get tough, it will be all okay in the end when a new day starts.
